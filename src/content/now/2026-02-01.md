title: The Aesthetic Shift
date: 2026-02-01
summary: Moving away from soft gradients toward a stark, terminal-inspired interface.
---
I realized the site was feeling too "modern web." Everything was too soft.

I'm leaning into:
- Hard edges (0px radius)
- Stamped shadows
- Monospace labels
- Structural grids

The "Now" section is the first to get this treatment.
