An abstracted landscape derived from repeated organic forms. The palette is constrained — earth tones and warm neutrals — with contrast built through value rather than hue.

This was the first in a series of small-format studies exploring pattern as landscape. Each mark is deliberate, each repetition slightly imperfect.
