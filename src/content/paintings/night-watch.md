An intersection at night. The scene is rendered from the perspective of someone standing still while the world moves around them. Car headlights blur into horizontal streaks. The buildings are felt more than seen.

Part of an ongoing series examining how we experience familiar spaces after dark — when context collapses and everything becomes texture.
